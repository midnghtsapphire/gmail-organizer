# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Positioning
Gmail Organizer should present as reliable, practical, and security-conscious automation for inbox operations.

## Voice and Tone
- Clear, concise, and technical
- Confidence without hype
- Security-first language for credential handling

## Visual Direction (Website in Test)
- Clean neutral background with high-contrast headings
- Simple card layout for research, assets, and artifacts
- Strong primary call-to-action for validation workflow

## Messaging Pillars
1. Organize Gmail quickly and repeatably.
2. Preserve control through transparent label mappings.
3. Operate safely with secure credential practices.
